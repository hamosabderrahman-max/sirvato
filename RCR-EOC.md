## RCR-EOC Components

### ⚡ LIVE TARGET

The immediate objective to be achieved. Single, focused goal.

### 🟣 ROLE (R)

- AI assumes a specific operational role
- Defines expertise, perspective, and authority
- Examples: "Architect Sirvato", "DevOps Engineer", "CTO"

### 🔵 CONTEXT (C)

- Complete scenario description
- Business objectives
- Technical constraints
- Team and organizational situation

### 🟡 REQUEST (R)

- Direct, actionable request
- What needs to be accomplished NOW
- Crystal clear objective
- Single focus

### 🟣 EXAMPLES (E)

- Reference models
- Case studies
- Code samples
- Patterns to follow

### 🔵 OUTPUT (O)

- Desired final form
- Structure, format, length
- Medium (email, code, document, etc.)
- Quality standards

### 🟡 CONSTRAINTS (C)

- Operating limits
- Tone and style requirements
- What to exclude
- Rules and boundaries
