# Governance Documentation for Sirvato

## Audit
Auditing in Sirvato ensures that all transactions and system activities are logged and can be reviewed. We implement comprehensive logging mechanisms that track changes, access, and data usage.

## Compliance
Sirvato adheres to industry regulations and standards, ensuring that all operations comply with legal and regulatory requirements. This includes data protection, user privacy, and financial reporting requirements.

## Reversibility
To maintain flexibility, Sirvato incorporates mechanisms to reverse transactions or changes if necessary. This feature helps in rectifying mistakes and enhances user trust in the platform.

## Immutable Logging Systems
Sirvato employs immutable logging systems to maintain the integrity of records. Once data is recorded, it cannot be altered or deleted, ensuring a trustworthy and transparent auditing trail.
