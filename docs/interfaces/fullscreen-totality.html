What is Fullscreen Totality?

Fullscreen Totality is the apex of the SIRVATO OS interface hierarchy. It combines the immersive canvas of Fullscreen Mode with all 15 modules of Master Block, arranged in a single, meticulously composed full-viewport layout that makes every data stream, control surface, and operational feed simultaneously visible.

No switching tabs. No toggling panels. No choosing between metrics and logs. In Totality, you see everything at once — compute, network, security, deployments, AI insights, cost, audit, and more — all live, all in one breath. It's the interface for when you need to understand your entire system in a single glance.

Total Awareness

Totality was designed for the most critical moments — major launches, incident war rooms, capacity planning sessions — where incomplete information is not an option. See everything. Miss nothing.

Configuration

YAML 
# sirvato.yaml — Fullscreen Totality
interfaces:
  fullscreen_totality:
    enabled: true
    trigger_key: "T"
    modules: "all"
    layout: "auto-compose"
    streams:
      refresh_rate: "250ms"
      sync_all: true
    master_control:
      enabled: true
      actions:
        - "scale"
        - "route"
        - "deploy"
        - "audit"
    accent: "gradient-magenta-cyan"
    multi_monitor: true
