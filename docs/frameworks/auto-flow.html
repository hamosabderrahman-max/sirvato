Auto-Flow

9-step sequential prompt construction from Role to Execute — structured, repeatable, reliable.

Components

  • Step 1 — Role Define the AI persona and expertise level.
  • Step 2 — Context Set the full situational context.
  • Step 3 — Request State the precise task.
  • Step 4 — Examples Provide models and references.
  • Step 5 — Output Define the output format.
  • Step 6 — Constraints Set the boundaries and limits.
  • Step 7 — ICDF Auto-structure with Instruction + Context + Data + Format.
  • Step 8 — Unified Fuse all frameworks into unified mode.
  • Step 9 — Execute Generate the final optimized prompt.

Example

🔄 AUTO-FLOW — SEQUENTIAL
────────────────────────────────────────
Step 1 — Role:        Marketing director, B2C e-commerce
Step 2 — Context:     Q4 campaign, €50k budget, EU market
Step 3 — Request:     Create a 6-week email sequence for cart abandonment
Step 4 — Examples:    Klaviyo best practices, urgency + social proof
Step 5 — Output:      6 email subjects + body outlines
Step 6 — Constraints: GDPR compliant, max 200 words/email
Step 7 — ICDF:        [auto-structured]
Step 8 — Unified:     [fused]
Step 9 — Execute ✅
────────────────────────────────────────
