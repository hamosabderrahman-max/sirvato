Unified Mode

All frameworks fused into the 11-component Sistema Unico — the most powerful prompt architecture in SIRVATO OS.

Components

  • 1. Role AI persona with expertise and authority.
  • 2. Instruction Precise action from ICDF.
  • 3. Message Core message from MICRO.
  • 4. Intention Double intention (effect + emotion).
  • 5. Request Specific deliverable from RCR-EOC.
  • 6. Context Full situational context.
  • 7. Data Source material and facts.
  • 8. Examples Models, references and counter-examples.
  • 9. Rhythm Tone, pace, energy and intensity.
  • 10. Constraints Hard limits and exclusions.
  • 11. Output Exact format and delivery specification.

Example

🧩 UNIFIED MODE — SISTEMA UNICO
════════════════════════════════════════
1.  Role:        Senior AI content strategist
2.  Instruction: Create a complete content calendar
3.  Message:     AI is the ultimate productivity multiplier
4.  Intention:   Drive engagement + establish authority
5.  Request:     12-week LinkedIn content plan
6.  Context:     B2B SaaS, 5k followers, growth phase
7.  Data:        Q1 analytics, top 5 posts, audience data
8.  Examples:    Justin Welsh, Lenny Rachitsky style
9.  Rhythm:      Strategic, insightful, action-oriented
10. Constraints: 3 posts/week, max 300 words, no fluff
11. Output:      Table: Date | Topic | Format | Hook
════════════════════════════════════════
