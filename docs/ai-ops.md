# Sirvato AI-Ops Operational Intelligence

## Overview
Sirvato AI-Ops provides a comprehensive suite of tools designed to enhance operational intelligence in IT environments. The solution encompasses various features tailored to improve system management and ensure optimal performance.

## Key Features

### Anomaly Detection
Sirvato's advanced anomaly detection capabilities utilize machine learning models to identify unusual patterns in operational data. This allows organizations to quickly pinpoint and respond to potential issues before they escalate into severe outages.

### Self-Healing
With self-healing functionality, Sirvato can automatically rectify minor issues without human intervention. By leveraging predefined rules and AI-driven insights, it ensures high availability and reduces downtime.

### Predictive Analytics
Sirvato employs predictive analytics to forecast potential problems and system performance degradations. By analyzing historical data, the platform can provide actionable insights that empower teams to make informed decisions and maintain system integrity.

### Observability Integration
Seamless observability integration is a key feature of Sirvato AI-Ops, enabling users to gain full visibility into their application stacks. By collecting metrics, logs, and traces, it offers a holistic view of system health and performance, facilitating quicker troubleshooting and enhancing operational efficiency.
