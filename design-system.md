# Design System

## AI-First Architecture
An AI-First architecture prioritizes the integration of artificial intelligence techniques and capabilities at the core of the systems and processes. This approach ensures that decision-making and operations are enhanced through machine learning algorithms, predictive analytics, and automated insights.

## Zero-Trust Architecture
Zero-Trust architecture assumes that threats could be internal or external and requires strict verification for every person and device trying to access resources. It emphasizes continuous authentication, minimal access privileges, and verification of real-time context to protect sensitive data.

## Self-Governing Principles
Self-Governing principles focus on the system's ability to autonomously manage its operations, compliance, and resource allocation, based on pre-defined rules and policies. This leads to improved efficiency, reduced manual oversight, and increased adaptiveness to changes in the environment.

## AUTOPILOT Operational Mode
The AUTOPILOT operational mode enables systems to operate with minimal human intervention. This mode utilizes machine learning and automation to make real-time adjustments, ensuring optimal performance and response to varying conditions without requiring constant human oversight.
